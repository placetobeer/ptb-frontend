What crazy thing is PlaceToBeer?

When deciding where to go in a friends group there are always discussions about which place to visit. This is an avoidable waste of time. To reduce this nonsense and create new memories PlaceToBeer will help you with that decision.

PlaceToBeer is a webbased platform to propose and rate places and events in your friend group. The idea is to individually rate bars, pubs, clubs, restaurants or wherever you can have fun together located in Karlsruhe.

To use our service you will need to register. Afterwards you can start creating a new group and your first proposals.
